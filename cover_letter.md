Subject: Application for the Position of Senior Staff Nurse

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to express my strong interest in the Senior Staff Nurse position at Unico Hospitals, as advertised in your recent job circular. With my professional qualification as a BNMC Registered Nurse and clinical experience specializing in Operation Theatre (OT), Post-Operative Care, and Emergency Care, I am confident in my ability to contribute effectively to the dedicated healthcare team at your 250-bed multi-specialty facility.

I hold a Diploma in Nursing Science & Midwifery from the IBN Sina Nursing Institute, recognized by the Bangladesh Nursing & Midwifery Council (BNMC Registration No: 102293), graduating with a CGPA of 3.83/4.00. These experiences have equipped me with strong clinical hands-on skills, including patient assessment, vital signs monitoring, IV cannulation, medication administration, and precise EMR/EHR clinical documentation. I am well-versed in maintaining a sterile environment, following strict infection control protocols, and collaborating effectively with multidisciplinary medical teams to provide compassionate, patient-centered care. 

I am highly motivated to join Unico Hospitals because of your reputation for providing outstanding, compassionate care ("Care, as unique as you"). I am eager to bring my nursing skills, dedication, and patient-first attitude to your esteemed hospital.

Please find attached my updated resume, BNMC registration certificate, and recent passport-size photograph for your kind review. I would appreciate the opportunity to discuss how my skills and experience can contribute to the nursing team at Unico Hospitals.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to your positive response.
